Researchers went public on realizing the fix was silent, releasing a PoC, and NVD later linked CVE-2026-31635. An email thread shows a DUPLICATE reply.

Only distros with CONFIG_RXGK enabled are affected: Fedora, Arch Linux, and openSUSE Tumbleweed, and in containers a vulnerable worker node can give a pod escape path. CVSS 7.5: the missing guard in rxgk_decrypt_skb writes to /etc/shadow, /etc/sudoers, and SUID binaries. Three distro logos glow red while others stay dark.

rxgk_decrypt_skb decrypts an incoming sk_buff whose pages are shared with other processes' page cache, where copy-on-write should protect shared pages but the COW guard was missing. Data lands in privileged memory or files, leading to LPE. A socket buffer is decrypted in place as plaintext bleeds into the /etc/shadow page cache.
