# AI Cybersecurity Carousel Image Production Brief

## Production Assumptions

The deliverables are optimized for Instagram-style carousel posts at **1080 × 1350 px portrait, 4:5 ratio**. The visual system should be cinematic, dark, high-contrast, and cybersecurity/editorial in tone. The cover backgrounds must contain **no rendered text** and should leave a clean dark lower-third for later typography. The finished carousel slides should include the requested text directly in the image generation output, with large readable typography and minimal copy per slide.

## Shared Style Direction

Use realistic editorial cybersecurity photography mixed with premium social carousel design. Prefer dark backgrounds, strong focal points, cyan/electric-blue/neon-green accents, and clean contrast. Avoid real logos, real credentials, readable private data, exploit payloads, or step-by-step abuse visuals.
